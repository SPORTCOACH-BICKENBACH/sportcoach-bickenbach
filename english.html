What does a Sport Coach do?

On behalf of the "Sport integriert Hessen" program, sport coaches can establish contacts between network partners and sports clubs and accompany the target group of the program (e.g. refugees, immigrants, low-income people, people with low education levels) - especially in the beginning - to sports activities.

Did you play sports in your home country and are looking for training opportunities here?

Or would you like to discover new sports? On this homepage you will find sports offers from clubs in Bickenbach.

Non-profit clubs are the best way to make social contacts in Germany. Here you can use German in everyday life and at the same time do something for your health through sports. Generally, anyone can become a member of a club and the monthly membership fees are usually affordable. As a member, you also have the chance to participate in shaping the club and take on responsibility.

If you cannot find a sport here, it may be available in the surrounding area.
